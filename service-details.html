    Hellen Social Work Services in Private Practice.

    We offer a range of professional counselling services designed to meet your needs, including Online Counselling for convenient virtual sessions, Home Visits for personalized care in the comfort of your home, and Office Counselling for private, face-to-face support in a safe environment. Our services are delivered by qualified professionals, registered under Practice Number 1255681 and Counselor Number 10-54719, ensuring ethical standards and quality care.

    • Confidentiality and Trust – Creating a safe space where clients feel secure to share openly.
    • Active Listening and Empathy – Understanding clients’ feelings and perspectives without judgment.
    • Goal-Oriented Approach – Setting clear objectives and providing practical strategies for positive change.

    Our counselling services from online, home visits, and in-office are designed to create real change from the very first session. We start with a warm, structured assessment, set clear goals together, and provide practical tools you can use right away. Whether it’s communication strategies, coping skills, or personalized action plans, you’ll leave each session with concrete steps, supportive resources, and a follow up plan so you don’t feel stuck. We believe in measurable progress, compassionate care, and ongoing support—so you never leave the session the way you came in.

    Thank you for considering our counselling services. We are committed to walking this journey with you and ensuring every session brings you closer to healing, growth, and clarity. Your well-being is our priority, and we look forward to meeting you soon because taking the first step toward support is the beginning of positive change.
